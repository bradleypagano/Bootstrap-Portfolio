About Me

I have to care about what I write here... So, I guess I better get this right. I mean, if I got this wrong, it would be devastating. I would prefer not to have to write about myself, but we all have to grow up sometime, right?

I majored in business management and began to work as a project manager in the 3D printing industry. After seven years of the same job in different comapnies, I got bored with the job and decided to move into another industry. Also, the fact that I got fired from the last 3D printing position I held was a good nudge. I then went into fiber construction. This only lasted a couple of years. I was bored almost immediately. There wasn't a lot of task, and I was idle most of the time. I felt as if my mind was melting away. Then, I decided to look into coding as something to learn in my downtime. I started to appreciate the challenge. It was almost as I was putting together a puzzle. I am now enrolled in a boot camp to jump into this new career with both feet. I'm being challenged every day, and it's a lot of fun.
